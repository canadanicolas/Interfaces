-Press "w" to perform a slow jump.

-Press "e" to perform a fast jump.

-You start with 5 lives.

-Ground enemy = -1 life, Flying enemy = -0.5 lives

-Each coin equals 10 points, get as many as you can.

-For every 10 coins you get an extra life!

TIP: You can go over or under the flying enemies

TIP: Play it with sound :)
